Black Jack rules and disclaimer

Rules

Objective

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without exceeding a total of 21.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10 points.
  • Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points, depending on which value helps the hand the most.

Game Play

  • Betting: Players place their bets.
  • Dealing: The dealer and player will start the game with one card.
  • Player's Turn: Starting with the player to the dealer's left, each player has options:

    • Hit: Take another card. Players can hit as many times as they want, but if they go over 21, they bust (lose).
    • Stay: Keep their current hand and end their turn.
    • Double Down: Double their bet and receive only one more card.

  • Dealer's Turn: After you; the players have completed your turn. The dealer must hit until their total is your score or higher without going Bust.
  • Winning: Players win if their hand total is higher than the dealer's without exceeding 21, or if the dealer busts. If the player and dealer have the same total, it's a Draw, and the player's bet is returned.

Payouts

  • Winning Hand: Typically pays 1:1.
  • Blackjack: A two-card hand of 21 (Ace + 10-value card) usually pays 3:2.

Strategy Tips

    Blackjack is a mix of luck and strategy, making it an exciting game, hope you enjoy!
